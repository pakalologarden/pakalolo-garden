# pakalolo.garden — Voice & Philosophy

## Core Philosophy
"Create the conditions. Let the plant do the rest."
Not: "Push the plant harder."

## Preferred Language
- plant health
- root-zone health
- genetic expression
- environmental optimization
- stress reduction
- plant vigor
- terpene expression
- consistency
- cultivation quality

## Avoid
- bigger yields
- explosive growth
- monster harvests
- maximum production
- insane yields

## The Root Philosophy
The goal is not simply growing bigger plants.
The goal is creating an environment where the plant
can fully express its genetics.

Roots are where that process begins.
Take care of the roots first.
When the roots thrive, the plant has the opportunity
to become the best version of itself genetically.
